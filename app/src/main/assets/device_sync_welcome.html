Welcome to AppSyncr!

AppSyncr gives you a single place to track all the apps you have installed across all of your android devices along with managing apps on your local device. With AppSyncr, you can clone a remote device to your local device, see what apps are missing on your local device compared to your other devices and more. AppSyncr supports both tablet and ATV.

 

You will next be asked to log in with your google account. AppSyncr does not collect any personally identifiable information - we simply collect and store the list of apps installed on your device along with an obfuscated user token. A google login, internet connectivity and google play services are required to use this application. Location is used for identifying where your various devices are (we only use course location info).

Note that if you have a lot of apps installed across your devices, performance may be a bit sluggish at first due to data synchronization to cloud being done in the background. Letting the device settle for a few minutes after logging in is recommended for users with large app loads.
