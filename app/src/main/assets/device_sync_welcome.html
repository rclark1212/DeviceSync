Welcome to the AppSyncr Alpha! (and eye chart :))

So what is AppSyncr? It gives you a single place to track all the apps you have installed across all of your android devices along with managing your local device. With AppSyncr, you can clone a remote device to your local device, see what apps are missing on your local device compared to your other devices and more.

AppSyncr supports both tablet and ATV. However it was primarily developed for ATV devices - most people (including myself) expect their TV’s to offer a consistent set of services regardless of the location and AppSyncr gives you an easy way to ensure a common application load across ATV devices.

You will next be asked to log in with your google account. AppSyncr does not collect any personally identifiable information - we simply collect and store the list of apps installed on your device along with a user token (this is not your email - it is an obfuscated token such as r8KezhhfF5RJ2rYTkLkId7xCjxT2). A google login, internet connectivity and google play services are required to use this application. Location is used for identifying where your various devices are (we only use course location info) - location, however, is not required.
